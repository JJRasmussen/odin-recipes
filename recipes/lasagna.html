Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Mushrooms
  • Black lentils
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Prepare the vegetables and start simmering them
  3. Add the lentils and spinach
  4. Add everything together and put in the pasta sheets
  5. Put in the oven for 25 minutes
