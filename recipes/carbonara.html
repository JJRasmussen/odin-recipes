Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino
  • Black pepper
  • Guanciale
  • Egg yolk
  • Garlic
  1. Start boiling some water in a pot. Use as little water as possible for the given amount of spaghetti.
  2. When the water boils put in the spaghetti, following the instructions on the package.
  3. Cut the guanciale into small pieces and heat it in a pan.
  4. Peel the garlic and crush it and cut it into tiny pieces.
  5. Reduce the heat on the guanciale and add the garlic.
  6. When the spaghetti is finished, move it to the pan, while keeping the water in the pot
  7. Grate the pecorino over the spaghetti.
  8. Give the carbonara plenty of freshly ground black pepper.
  9. Pour some of the water from the pot over the spaghetti. It helps creating a cheesy sauce with the pecorino.
  10. Lower the heat. When it is low the egg should be added.
  11. Stir everything and plate it neatly.
  12. Consider grating additional pecorino on top.
